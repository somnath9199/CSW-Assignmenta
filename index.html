History

World Cup 2019

The 2019 ICC Cricket World Cup was hosted by England and Wales, marking the fifth time the tournament was held in these countries. England won the tournament, defeating New Zealand in the final based on the boundary count rule...


World Cup 2015

The 2015 Cricket World Cup was co-hosted by Australia and New Zealand. Australia won their fifth World Cup by defeating New Zealand in the final...


World Cup 2011

The 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup was jointly hosted by India, Sri Lanka, and Bangladesh. India lifted the trophy for the second time by defeating Sri Lanka in the final...
